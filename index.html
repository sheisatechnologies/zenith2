Welcome to Amrit Vidya Ashram

The school is established by the Amrit Nath Education Trust, Kolkata and managed by the registered management committee of Amrit Vidya Ashram, headquartered at Fatehpur, Sikar. Guided by the noble vision of Mahant Narhari Nath, the school focuses on delivering strong spiritual and moral education built on a secular and inclusive foundation.

Learn More

Amrit Vidya Ashram is located in Fatehpur, a unique and culturally rich town in the Sikar district of Rajasthan. Situated on the Jaipur–Bikaner National Highway (NH 11), Fatehpur - known for its historical significance and stunning heritage sites, Fatehpur attracts visitors from across India and around the world. The town has played a notable role in shaping the historical, cultural, and economic landscape of Rajasthan and continues to be a centre of vibrant traditional art and architecture.

Admission Procedure

At the Pre - Primary level students are admitted on the basis of first come first served for the number of sheets available. Age limit for class Nursery is 2.5 on 1st April of the year at the time of Joining the school. For class one onward students are required to appear for an entrance test. This test is held in March for admission in the following academic session. While the entrance test form the basis of selection it is not the sole criterion and does not guarantee admission.
